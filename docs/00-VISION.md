# AgentBloom — Product Vision

## Name: AgentBloom
**Tagline:** "Speak it. Built it. Bloom."
**Value Proposition:** The voice-first AI platform that turns one sentence into a fully built, hosted, and managed online business — pages, funnels, courses, emails, bookings, and more — without touching code or hiring anyone.

## Why "AgentBloom"
- **Agent** = AI-powered autonomous agent that does the work
- **Bloom** = Growth metaphor — your business blooms from a single seed (a voice command)
- Memorable, friendly, non-technical — appeals to coaches, tradespeople, creators alike
- Domain-friendly, brandable, unique

## Core Concept
An all-in-one, voice-first, agentic platform that lets non-technical users in any niche build, host, and manage high-converting landing pages, funnels, online courses/memberships, email lists, booking systems, and private member areas — all through natural voice/text conversation with a dedicated AI agent.

## Target Users
| Segment | Examples | Primary Need |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Local Services | HVAC, plumbing, electricians, salons, repair shops | Lead gen, booking, local SEO |
| Wellness/Coaching | Yoga teachers, life coaches, fitness instructors | Course sales, memberships, booking |
| Online Creators | Course creators, digital product sellers, bloggers | Paywalls, content delivery, email lists |
| Small Business | Restaurants, real estate agents, event planners, freelancers | Online presence, reservations, portfolios |

## What Makes This Different
1. **Voice-first**: Not just a chatbot — a true voice-controlled business builder
2. **Agentic, not template-only**: Agent reasons, adapts, learns your business context
3. **Knowledge-base powered**: Upload your docs/videos/audio → agent uses YOUR expertise for all content
4. **Full content generation**: Every page comes complete — copy, images, testimonials, pricing — not blank templates
5. **All-in-one**: No need for 5 different SaaS subscriptions
6. **In-house tools**: Not gluing together third-party widgets — built for cohesion

## Key User Flow
1. Sign up → connect/transfer custom domain → authenticate Google account
2. Receive personalized dashboard + dedicated AI agent instance
3. Upload business knowledge base (PDFs, Word, PPT, text, voice, video → all processed)
4. Speak or type commands → agent builds, previews, deploys
5. Ongoing: agent manages content, emails, analytics, member onboarding
